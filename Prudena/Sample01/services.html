We believe there is a better way.

Let’s face it, doing in-depth equity analysis is a time-consuming, high-skill activity. Not everyone has all the tools to do it well. However, together, we can dramatically reduce the time and effort required to get the insights that we need to make high-quality investment decisions. Wikipedia showed how the crowd can beat the “experts” and produce something incredible. The open-source software revolution revealed how transparency and collaboration can improve quality. The dramatic reduction in cost of financial data and the development of APIs have made it easier than ever to aggregate the data required to perform financial analysis. We believe that the time has come to combine these ideas and new technologies to save everyone time and improve the quality of our financial decisions.

Equity Valuation for Everyone?

Yes, that is right— analysis for everyone. When it comes to investing in an individual stock or a sector ETF, it is critical to ground yourself in the economic fundamentals of that business or sector. Benjamin Graham introduced this idea when he wrote about assessing a company's "intrinsic value" to determine your "margin of safety." Unfortunately, it is one thing to think it is a good idea to do quantitative analysis and quite another to actually do it. We created PRUDENA to make analyzing stocks easier so that you users better understand (and are more comfortable with) the the risks that are inherent in any investment decision. We recognize that the future is uncertain and often unpredictable. One of the central goals of PRUDENA is to make it easy for you to assess ranges for possible stock values rather than focusing on one intrinsic value. We believe that it is better recognize and plan for uncertainly rather than pretending we have a crystal ball. We are truly dedicated to Erwin Schrödinger's idea of providing "clarity through fuzziness." PRUDENA – wiser together.
